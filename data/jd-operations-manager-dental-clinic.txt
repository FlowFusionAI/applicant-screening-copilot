OPERATIONS MANAGER — MULTI-SITE DENTAL GROUP
Lakeshore Smiles Dental Group — Chicago, IL (on-site) | Full-time
Posted: June 2026

WHO WE ARE
Lakeshore Smiles is a family-owned dental group with three locations across
Chicago's North Side, 6 dentists, 9 hygienists, and a front-office team of 14.
We see roughly 2,400 patient visits a month and are opening a fourth location
in early 2027.

THE ROLE
We need an Operations Manager to run the day-to-day business side of all
three clinics so our clinicians can focus on patients. You'll own scheduling
efficiency, front-office staffing, insurance/billing oversight, compliance,
and vendor relationships, reporting directly to the practice owner.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Oversee front-office operations across three locations: scheduling,
  check-in/out flow, recall systems, and patient communications
- Optimize provider schedules to reduce no-shows and unfilled chair time;
  own the no-show rate and chair-utilization KPIs
- Supervise, hire, onboard, and schedule a front-office team of 14
- Oversee insurance verification, claims submission, and A/R follow-up with
  our billing coordinator; escalate and resolve denied-claim patterns
- Maintain HIPAA and OSHA compliance: training records, audits,
  incident reporting
- Manage vendor contracts and supply ordering across sites; keep supply
  spend within budget
- Prepare monthly operations reports (production, collections, new-patient
  numbers) for the owner
- Lead the operational setup of our fourth location (2027)

REQUIREMENTS
- 3+ years of operations or practice management experience, healthcare or
  dental strongly preferred
- Working knowledge of dental practice management software (Dentrix,
  Eaglesoft, or Open Dental)
- Understanding of dental insurance: PPO fee schedules, claims, EOBs,
  pre-authorizations
- Experience hiring, scheduling, and managing hourly staff
- Comfortable with Excel/Google Sheets for reporting and budgeting
- Calm, organized, and decisive under a busy clinic's daily chaos

NICE TO HAVE
- Dental assisting or front-desk background (you've sat in the chair-side
  and front-desk seats yourself)
- Multi-site management experience
- Spanish proficiency
- Experience with a de novo clinic opening

COMPENSATION
$70,000–$85,000 depending on experience, quarterly bonus tied to
collections and utilization targets, health insurance, 3 weeks PTO, free
dental care for you and immediate family.

Lakeshore Smiles Dental Group is an equal opportunity employer.
